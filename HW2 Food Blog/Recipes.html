STEPS

Heat oven to 375 degress

Place the tin tomatoes, basil, and diced up garlic clove in a saucepan. Cook over medium heat for 20-30 minutes until the tomatoes have broken down and the sauce has thickened. Leave to cool. Spread the pizza sauce over the pizza base and top with sliced tomatoes and mozzarella. Drizzle over olive oil and salt + pepper. Cook on a pizza stone in an extremely hot oven until the toppings have cooked and the base has risen and browned. Leave to cool for a few minutes before garnishing with the basil leaves. Serve immediately.
